QuillBot

QuillBot is an AI-powered paraphrasing and grammar checking tool that helps millions of students, writers, and professionals cut their writing time by more than half.

Uses of QuillBot

  • Paraphrasing text in different styles (Standard, Fluency, Creative, Formal, Shorten, Expand)
  • Checking grammar and spelling
  • Summarizing long pieces of text
  • Generating citations in various styles (APA, MLA, Chicago)
  • Comparing different versions of paraphrased text
  • Improving vocabulary and writing style

How to Use QuillBot

  1. Go to the QuillBot website.
  2. You can either paste your text directly into the input box on the website or use their Chrome extension.
  3. Choose a paraphrasing mode based on your desired outcome.
  4. Click the "Paraphrase" button.
  5. QuillBot will generate rewritten versions of your text.
  6. Review the suggestions and edit them as needed.
  7. Use the Grammar Checker to identify and correct any errors in your text.
  8. Utilize the Summarizer to get a concise overview of longer content.
  9. Use the Citation Generator to create accurate citations for your work.

Paid/Free Status

QuillBot offers a free plan with limitations on the number of words that can be paraphrased and summarized at once. They also have premium subscription plans that offer higher word limits, access to all paraphrasing modes, and other advanced features.
