AVCLabs Video Enhancer AI

AVCLabs Video Enhancer AI is a powerful desktop software that utilizes artificial intelligence algorithms to enhance and upscale video quality. It can increase video resolution up to 8K, reduce noise, sharpen details, stabilize shaky footage, colorize black and white videos, and even enhance faces.

Uses of AVCLabs Video Enhancer AI

  • Upscaling low-resolution videos (SD, HD) to higher resolutions (HD, 4K, 8K) with AI-powered detail enhancement.
  • Improving video quality by reducing noise, grain, and artifacts.
  • Sharpening blurry videos and enhancing overall clarity.
  • Stabilizing shaky video footage for smoother playback.
  • Colorizing black and white videos automatically using AI.
  • Enhancing facial details and recovering clarity in faces.
  • Deinterlacing videos to remove motion artifacts and flickering.
  • Converting video formats between various popular codecs (e.g., MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV).
  • Adjusting video brightness, contrast, saturation, and other color properties.
  • Removing unwanted objects or blurring faces for privacy.

How to Use AVCLabs Video Enhancer AI

  1. Go to the AVCLabs website and download and install the software compatible with your operating system (Windows or macOS). They offer a free trial version.
  2. Purchase a license for the full version to unlock all features and remove limitations.
  3. Open AVCLabs Video Enhancer AI.
  4. Click the "Browse" button to add the video file(s) you want to enhance. Batch processing is supported.
  5. Choose an AI model based on your needs:
    • **AI Enhancement:** For general video quality improvement and upscaling.
    • **Face Refinement:** To enhance facial details in the video.
    • **Colorize:** To add color to black and white videos.
    • **Motion Compensation:** To smooth out motion and reduce flicker.
    • **Denoise:** To reduce noise and grain in the video.
  6. Configure the output settings, including resolution, format, codec, and bitrate. You can choose from presets or customize the settings.
  7. Adjust other parameters like brightness, contrast, and saturation if needed.
  8. (Optional) Use the trimming and cropping tools for basic video editing.
  9. Click the "Start Processing" button to begin the video enhancement process. The processing time will depend on the video length, resolution, and chosen AI models.
  10. Preview the enhanced video and save it to your desired location.
