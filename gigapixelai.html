Topaz Gigapixel AI

Topaz Gigapixel AI is a specialized AI-powered software designed to upscale images to higher resolutions while adding natural-looking detail and sharpness. Unlike traditional upscaling methods that can result in blurry or pixelated images, Gigapixel AI uses deep learning to intelligently fill in missing information and create stunningly detailed enlargements.

Uses of Topaz Gigapixel AI

  • Upscaling low-resolution photos for printing at larger sizes
  • Enlarging images for use in high-resolution displays
  • Recovering detail in old or low-quality photographs
  • Enhancing the resolution of images for professional use
  • Improving the quality of upscaled images from other software
  • Creating high-resolution assets for graphic design and web use
  • Preparing images for AI art generation workflows

How to Use Topaz Gigapixel AI

  1. Go to the Topaz Labs website and download and install Gigapixel AI. They often offer a free trial.
  2. Purchase a license if you want to continue using the software after the trial.
  3. Open Topaz Gigapixel AI.
  4. Drag and drop or open the image you want to upscale.
  5. Choose your desired scaling factor (e.g., 2x, 4x, custom dimensions).
  6. Select an AI model optimized for your image type (e.g., Standard, Lines, Art & CG, Low Resolution). The software often recommends a model.
  7. Adjust the "Suppress Noise" and "Remove Blur" sliders as needed to refine the upscaling process.
  8. Use the "Face Refinement" option if you are upscaling portraits to improve facial details.
  9. Preview the upscaled image in real-time to compare it with the original. You can zoom in to see the added detail.
  10. Adjust the settings until you are satisfied with the result.
  11. Click the "Save Image" button to export the upscaled image in your preferred format (e.g., JPG, PNG, TIFF).

Paid/Free Status

Topaz Gigapixel AI is a **paid software**. They offer a one-time purchase license. They typically provide a **free trial version** that allows you to test the software's capabilities with your own images before purchasing.
