Play.ht

Play.ht is an AI voice generator and text-to-speech platform that creates realistic human-like voiceovers in various languages and accents.

Uses of Play.ht

  • Creating voiceovers for videos and presentations
  • Generating audio for podcasts and audiobooks
  • Developing voice applications and assistants
  • Localizing content with multilingual voiceovers
  • Creating e-learning modules with engaging audio
  • Adding voice to animations and games
  • Generating audio for accessibility purposes

How to Use Play.ht

  1. Go to the Play.ht website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Navigate to the text-to-speech editor.
  4. Enter or paste your text that you want to convert to speech.
  5. Choose from a wide selection of AI voices in different languages and accents.
  6. Customize voice parameters such as speed, pitch, and emphasis.
  7. Preview the generated audio.
  8. Generate and download your audio file in various formats (e.g., MP3, WAV).
  9. Explore additional features like pronunciation editing and SSML controls for more advanced customization.

Paid/Free Status

Play.ht offers different subscription plans, including a free plan with limited text-to-speech generation. Paid plans provide access to more voices, longer audio generation times, and advanced features for personal and commercial use.
