Tome App

Tome is an AI-powered storytelling format that helps you create compelling and interactive narratives. It combines the ease of presentation software with the immersive quality of a website, enhanced by AI capabilities.

Uses of Tome App

  • Creating engaging presentations and pitch decks
  • Developing interactive stories and narratives
  • Building lightweight websites and portfolios
  • Sharing ideas and concepts visually
  • Collaborating with teams on storytelling projects
  • Embedding rich media (videos, 3D models, live data)
  • Generating content and layouts with AI assistance
  • Creating shareable and easily accessible content

How to Use Tome App

  1. Go to the Tome App website and create an account or log in.
  2. Start a new "tome" (a narrative or presentation).
  3. Use the intuitive interface to add "pages" and various content blocks (text, images, videos, embeds, etc.).
  4. Leverage the AI features to generate outlines, text content, and even suggest layouts based on your topic.
  5. Embed interactive elements like live data, 3D models, and web links.
  6. Customize the visual design with themes and styling options.
  7. Collaborate with others by sharing your tome and inviting them to edit.
  8. Present your tome directly from the platform or share a link.
  9. Analyze viewer engagement with built-in analytics.

Paid/Free Status

Tome App offers a free plan with certain limitations on the number of tomes and features. They also have paid subscription plans (Pro and Team) that unlock more features, increase the number of tomes you can create, offer more advanced AI capabilities, and provide team collaboration tools.
