Fotor

Fotor is a comprehensive online photo editor and graphic design tool that offers a wide range of features, including AI-powered enhancements, basic and advanced editing tools, collage maker, and design templates.

Uses of Fotor

  • Basic photo editing (cropping, resizing, rotating)
  • Advanced photo retouching (blemish removal, skin smoothing)
  • Applying filters and effects
  • Adjusting brightness, contrast, saturation, and color
  • Creating photo collages with various layouts
  • Designing graphics for social media, marketing materials, and more
  • AI-powered image enhancement and upscaling
  • AI background removal and object removal
  • AI art generation and effects
  • Creating HDR images

How to Use Fotor

  1. Go to the Fotor website.
  2. You can start editing or designing without creating an account, but signing up unlocks more features and saves your work.
  3. **Photo Editor:** Upload your image or paste a URL. Use the tools on the left sidebar for basic adjustments, effects, retouching, and AI enhancements.
  4. **Collage Maker:** Choose a layout or create a free-style collage. Upload your photos and arrange them in the editor. Customize borders, backgrounds, and add text or stickers.
  5. **Design Maker:** Browse through the templates for various purposes (social media posts, posters, invitations, etc.) or start with a blank canvas. Use the drag-and-drop interface to add text, images, shapes, and customize the design.
  6. Explore the AI tools for one-click enhancements, background removal, object removal, and generating AI art from text prompts (if available in your plan).
  7. Save your edited photos or designs in various formats (JPG, PNG, PDF).

Paid/Free Status

Fotor offers a **free version** with basic editing and design features. However, many advanced tools, AI features, and a wider range of templates and resources are available with a **Fotor Pro** or **Fotor Pro+** subscription. These paid plans offer benefits like higher resolution downloads, ad-free experience, and more cloud storage.
