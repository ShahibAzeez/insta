Pictory.ai

Pictory.ai is an AI-powered video editing tool that allows you to easily create and edit videos from text, scripts, blog posts, and even long videos.

Uses of Pictory.ai

  • Creating short social media videos from long-form content
  • Generating video summaries
  • Adding captions and subtitles automatically
  • Creating highlight reels
  • Turning blog posts into engaging videos
  • Editing videos using text transcripts

How to Use Pictory.ai

  1. Go to the Pictory.ai website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Choose the type of video you want to create (e.g., script to video, article to video, edit videos using text).
  4. Upload your text, paste a link, or upload your video.
  5. Pictory's AI will analyze your content and generate a video draft.
  6. Customize the video by adding visuals, music, text, and more using their intuitive editor.
  7. Preview and download your video.

Paid/Free Status

Pictory.ai offers various subscription plans with different features and usage limits. They often provide a free trial or a limited free plan to get started.
