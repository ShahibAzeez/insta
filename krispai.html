Krisp AI

Krisp AI is an AI-powered noise cancellation application that intelligently filters out background noise and echo from your microphone and speaker during online meetings, calls, and recordings.

Uses of Krisp AI

  • Removing background noise (e.g., keyboard typing, pets, traffic) from your microphone
  • Eliminating echo from your speaker
  • Improving the clarity of your voice for other participants
  • Ensuring a professional and distraction-free communication experience
  • Working seamlessly with various communication and collaboration platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, etc.)
  • Enhancing the quality of voice recordings and podcasts

How to Use Krisp AI

  1. Go to the Krisp AI website and download the application for your operating system (Windows or macOS).
  2. Create an account or log in after installation.
  3. Grant Krisp access to your microphone and speaker.
  4. Open your preferred communication or recording application (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet).
  5. In the audio settings of that application, select **Krisp Microphone** as your microphone input and **Krisp Speaker** as your speaker output.
  6. Krisp will now automatically filter out noise from your microphone and echo from your speaker during your calls and meetings.
  7. You can adjust Krisp's settings within the Krisp application interface if needed.

Paid/Free Status

Krisp AI offers a free plan with a limited number of noise cancellation minutes per week. They also have various paid subscription plans (Pro, Business) that provide unlimited noise cancellation, additional features, and support for more users.
