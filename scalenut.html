Scalenut

Scalenut is an AI-powered content marketing platform designed to help you plan, research, write, and optimize high-ranking content at scale.

Uses of Scalenut

  • Keyword research and analysis
  • Content planning and strategy
  • AI-powered content creation (blog posts, articles, product descriptions, etc.)
  • SEO optimization and recommendations
  • Content auditing and analysis
  • Competitor analysis
  • Generating content briefs

How to Use Scalenut

  1. Go to the Scalenut website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Start by using the keyword research tools to identify relevant and high-potential keywords for your niche.
  4. Utilize the content planner to strategize your content calendar and identify content gaps.
  5. Use the AI Writer to generate content based on your chosen keywords and content briefs. You can guide the AI with specific instructions and desired outcomes.
  6. Optimize your content for SEO using Scalenut's recommendations for keywords, headings, and other on-page elements.
  7. Analyze existing content on your website to identify areas for improvement.
  8. Research your competitors' content strategies to gain insights.
  9. Generate detailed content briefs to guide your writing process or for outsourcing.

Paid/Free Status

Scalenut offers various subscription plans with different features and usage limits, catering to individuals, startups, and large teams. They typically do not offer a completely free plan, but may provide free trials or limited-time offers to explore the platform.
