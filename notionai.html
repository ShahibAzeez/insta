Notion AI

Notion AI is an integrated AI assistant within the Notion workspace, designed to help you write faster, brainstorm ideas, summarize information, and more, directly within your notes and documents.

Uses of Notion AI

  • Generating different types of content (blog posts, meeting agendas, social media updates, etc.)
  • Brainstorming ideas and outlining content
  • Summarizing long documents and articles
  • Translating text into different languages
  • Improving writing quality and style
  • Extracting action items from notes
  • Creating tables and lists

How to Use Notion AI

  1. Go to the Notion website and log in to your workspace.
  2. Open a Notion page or create a new one.
  3. Type `/ai` to bring up the Notion AI menu, or select text and choose "Ask AI".
  4. Enter your prompt or question describing what you need help with (e.g., "Write a blog post about the benefits of remote work").
  5. Notion AI will generate a response based on your prompt.
  6. You can refine the output by asking for it to be longer, shorter, more formal, etc.
  7. Integrate the generated content into your Notion page.
  8. Experiment with different AI commands and features within your workspace.

Paid/Free Status

Notion AI is an add-on feature to your existing Notion subscription. It is not included in the free Notion plan. You need to subscribe to a paid Notion plan to access Notion AI, and there may be additional costs based on usage or credits.
