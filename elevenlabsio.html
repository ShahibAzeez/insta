ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI-powered voice generator that allows you to create highly realistic and expressive voices from text. It offers a range of pre-made voices and the ability to clone your own voice or create entirely new synthetic voices.

Uses of ElevenLabs

  • Generating voiceovers for videos and presentations
  • Creating audiobooks and podcasts with natural-sounding narration
  • Developing AI characters with unique voices for games and virtual environments
  • Producing realistic speech for accessibility tools
  • Localizing audio content into different languages with natural accents
  • Creating custom voices for branding and marketing purposes
  • Experimenting with creative audio projects and voice design

How to Use ElevenLabs

  1. Go to the ElevenLabs website and create an account or log in.
  2. Navigate to the "Text to Speech" section.
  3. Choose from the library of available AI voices or explore options to clone a voice or create a new one.
  4. Enter the text you want the AI to speak in the text input field.
  5. Adjust settings such as voice style, stability, and clarity to fine-tune the output.
  6. Click the "Generate" button to create the audio.
  7. Listen to the generated audio and make any necessary adjustments to the text or settings.
  8. Download the audio file in your preferred format (e.g., MP3).
  9. Explore other features like "Voice Cloning" (if you have a subscription that allows it) and the "Projects" section for managing your audio creations.

Paid/Free Status

ElevenLabs offers a free plan with a limited number of characters per month and access to a basic set of voices. They also have various paid subscription plans (Starter, Creator, Independent Publisher, Growing Business, Enterprise) that offer higher character limits, more features (like voice cloning and commercial usage rights), and priority support.
