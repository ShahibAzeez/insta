Descript

Descript is an all-in-one audio and video editing tool that uses AI to make editing as easy as working with a text document.

Uses of Descript

  • Transcribing audio and video automatically
  • Editing audio and video by editing text
  • Removing filler words (um, uh, etc.) with AI
  • Creating short video clips for social media
  • Adding captions and subtitles automatically
  • Overdubbing audio with realistic AI voices
  • Collaborating on audio and video projects

How to Use Descript

  1. Go to the Descript website.
  2. Download and install the Descript application on your computer.
  3. Create an account or log in.
  4. Start a new project and upload your audio or video files.
  5. Descript will automatically transcribe your media.
  6. Edit your audio and video by editing the text transcript. Deleting text removes the corresponding audio/video.
  7. Use AI features like Filler Word Removal and Overdub.
  8. Add captions, titles, and other visual elements.
  9. Mix your audio and finalize your video.
  10. Export your edited audio or video.

Paid/Free Status

Descript offers different subscription plans, including a free plan with limited transcription time and features. Paid plans provide more transcription hours, advanced AI features, and higher export quality.
