Wordtune

Wordtune is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you rewrite and rephrase your sentences to improve clarity, fluency, and impact.

Uses of Wordtune

  • Rewriting sentences for better clarity and style
  • Finding alternative ways to express your ideas
  • Adjusting the tone of your writing (formal, casual)
  • Shortening or expanding sentences
  • Checking for grammar and spelling errors
  • Translating text between languages
  • Improving overall writing quality

How to Use Wordtune

  1. Go to the Wordtune website or install their browser extension.
  2. Select the text you want to rewrite.
  3. Click the Wordtune icon that appears.
  4. Wordtune will provide several alternative ways to phrase your selected text.
  5. Choose the option that best fits your intended meaning and style.
  6. You can further refine the suggestions or try rewriting different parts of your text.
  7. Use the "Tone" and "Length" options to adjust the output.
  8. Utilize the Translate feature to translate text to or from multiple languages.

Paid/Free Status

Wordtune offers a free plan with a limited number of rewrites per day. They also have premium subscription plans that offer unlimited rewrites, access to all features (like tone and formality adjustments), and priority support.
