Lensa AI

Lensa AI is a mobile photo editing application that uses artificial intelligence to enhance your selfies and portraits, and create unique AI avatars.

Uses of Lensa AI

  • Retouching selfies and portraits (skin smoothing, blemish removal, eye enhancement)
  • Applying artistic filters and effects
  • Changing backgrounds of photos
  • Creating "Magic Avatars" from your uploaded photos in various artistic styles
  • Stylizing hair and adding makeup effects
  • Adjusting focus and depth of field
  • Enhancing overall image quality

How to Use Lensa AI

  1. Download and install the Lensa AI app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Open the Lensa AI app on your mobile device.
  3. Upload a photo you want to edit or take a new one within the app.
  4. Use the various editing tools and features available in the app.
  5. For "Magic Avatars," upload a set of your selfies following the app's instructions.
  6. Choose the style and number of avatars you want to generate.
  7. The AI will process your photos and create unique avatars.
  8. Save your edited photos or generated avatars to your device or share them on social media.
  9. Explore the different filters, effects, and customization options within the app.

Paid/Free Status

Lensa AI typically offers a free trial period with limited access to features. After the trial, it operates on a subscription basis for full access to all tools, filters, and the "Magic Avatars" feature. The cost of the subscription may vary.
