Luminar AI (Now Luminar Neo)

Luminar AI was a revolutionary photo editing software focused on artificial intelligence to simplify complex editing tasks and deliver stunning results quickly. It has since been succeeded and enhanced by **Luminar Neo**, which builds upon its AI capabilities with a modular engine for greater flexibility and performance. The core AI features and philosophy remain central to Luminar Neo.

Uses of Luminar AI / Luminar Neo

  • AI-powered sky replacement with realistic blending
  • Intelligent portrait enhancement (skin smoothing, eye brightening, face sculpting)
  • Automatic landscape enhancement with AI Accent and Golden Hour tools
  • Effortless object removal with AI Erase
  • Creative filters and looks powered by AI
  • Relighting photos with AI to adjust exposure and mood
  • Upscaling and enhancing image details with AI
  • Noise reduction and sharpening powered by AI
  • Streamlined workflow for both beginner and professional photographers

How to Use Luminar AI / Luminar Neo

  1. Go to the Skylum website and download and install Luminar Neo (or Luminar AI if you are using an older version).
  2. Purchase a license or start a trial if you haven't already.
  3. Open Luminar Neo and import the photos you want to edit.
  4. Explore the "Edit" panel, which contains various AI-powered tools organized into categories like Essentials, Creative, and Professional.
  5. Use the AI tools like "Enhance AI" for quick overall improvements, "Sky AI" to replace or enhance the sky, "Portrait AI" for retouching portraits, and "Landscape AI" for optimizing outdoor scenes.
  6. Apply creative "Looks" from the "Looks" panel for one-click stylistic edits.
  7. Utilize the "Erase" tool to remove unwanted objects seamlessly.
  8. Experiment with other AI-powered features and manual adjustments to achieve your desired look.
  9. Export your edited photos in various formats and sizes.

Paid/Free Status

Luminar Neo is a **paid software** with different licensing options (one-time purchase or subscription, depending on the region and current offers). Skylum typically offers a **free trial** period to allow users to test the software before purchasing. Luminar AI (the predecessor) was also a paid software and is no longer actively developed or sold, with users often encouraged to upgrade to Luminar Neo.
