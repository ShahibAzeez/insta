Synthesia.io

Synthesia.io is an AI video generation platform that allows you to create videos with AI avatars from just text. It's designed for professional video production at scale.

Uses of Synthesia.io

  • Creating training videos and tutorials
  • Generating product demos and explainers
  • Producing marketing and sales videos
  • Localizing content with multilingual AI avatars
  • Creating internal communication videos
  • Developing e-learning materials
  • Automating video creation workflows

How to Use Synthesia.io

  1. Go to the Synthesia.io website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Start a new video project.
  4. Choose an AI avatar from their diverse library or create your own custom avatar.
  5. Input your script or upload a voiceover.
  6. Customize the video with backgrounds, text, images, and other elements.
  7. Select the language and voice for your AI avatar.
  8. Generate the video. Synthesia's AI will animate the avatar to speak your script naturally.
  9. Review and edit the video as needed.
  10. Download your professional-quality AI video.

Paid/Free Status

Synthesia.io is a subscription-based platform primarily aimed at businesses and enterprises. They offer various pricing plans based on usage, features, and the number of users. They typically do not offer a free plan, but may provide demos or trials for potential customers.
