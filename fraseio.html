Frase.io

Frase.io was an AI-powered content optimization tool designed to help users research, write, and optimize high-ranking content for search engines. It has since been acquired by Jasper (formerly Jarvis.ai).

Uses of Frase.io (as a standalone tool)

  • Analyzing top-ranking content for target keywords
  • Generating content briefs and outlines
  • Providing AI-powered writing assistance to create SEO-friendly content
  • Optimizing existing content for better search engine rankings
  • Identifying key questions to answer in your content
  • Comparing your content to competitors' content

How to Use Frase.io (features now within Jasper)

While Frase.io is no longer available as a separate platform, its core functionalities are now integrated into Jasper. To utilize these features, you would typically:

  1. Go to the Jasper website and log in to your account.
  2. Start a new document or project.
  3. Use Jasper's research tools (which incorporate Frase's analysis capabilities) to analyze top-ranking content for your target keywords.
  4. Generate content briefs and outlines based on the research.
  5. Utilize Jasper's AI writing templates and commands to create SEO-optimized content, drawing on the insights gained from the research.
  6. Use Jasper's optimization features to refine your content for better search engine performance.

Paid/Free Status

As Frase.io is now part of Jasper, its features are included within Jasper's subscription plans. Jasper offers various paid plans with different features and usage limits. They may offer a free trial to explore the platform.
