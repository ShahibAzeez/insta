Grammarly

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you improve your writing by checking for grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, engagement, and delivery mistakes.

Uses of Grammarly

  • Checking and correcting grammar and spelling errors
  • Improving punctuation and sentence structure
  • Enhancing clarity and conciseness of writing
  • Adjusting tone to suit your audience and purpose
  • Providing suggestions for word choice and style
  • Checking for plagiarism (in premium version)
  • Offering feedback on engagement and delivery
  • Assisting with writing across various platforms (documents, emails, social media, etc.)

How to Use Grammarly

  1. Go to the Grammarly website and create a free account or log in.
  2. You can use Grammarly in several ways:
    • **Grammarly Web Editor:** Paste or type your text directly into the Grammarly editor on the website.
    • **Grammarly Browser Extension:** Install the Grammarly browser extension for Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. It will automatically check your writing in text boxes across the web (e.g., email, social media).
    • **Grammarly Desktop App:** Download and install the Grammarly desktop app for Windows or macOS to check documents directly on your computer.
    • **Grammarly for Microsoft Office:** Integrate Grammarly with Microsoft Word and Outlook.
    • **Grammarly Keyboard (Mobile):** Use the Grammarly keyboard on your iOS or Android device for real-time writing assistance in apps.
  3. As you write, Grammarly will highlight errors and offer suggestions for correction and improvement.
  4. Click on the highlighted suggestions to see explanations and apply the corrections.
  5. Adjust your writing goals (formality, tone, intent, audience) to get more tailored feedback.
  6. Review the overall performance of your text based on Grammarly's insights.

Paid/Free Status

Grammarly offers a free version that checks for basic grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors. They also have premium subscription plans (Grammarly Premium and Grammarly Business) that offer more advanced features such as:

  • Clarity-focused rewrites
  • Tone adjustments
  • Vocabulary enhancement suggestions
  • Plagiarism detection
  • Fluency checks
  • Additional advanced suggestions

Grammarly Business also includes team-based features like style guides and analytics.
