DALL-E 2

DALL-E 2 is a new AI system that can create realistic images and art from descriptions in natural language.

Uses of DALL-E 2

  • Generating unique images from text prompts
  • Creating variations of existing images
  • Expanding images beyond their original borders (outpainting)
  • Making realistic edits to existing images based on text instructions (inpainting)
  • Combining different visual concepts
  • Brainstorming visual ideas
  • Creating custom illustrations and designs

How to Use DALL-E 2

  1. Go to the DALL-E 2 website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Enter a detailed text prompt describing the image you want to generate (e.g., "a corgi riding a bicycle through a futuristic city").
  4. Click the "Generate" button.
  5. DALL-E 2 will generate several image options based on your prompt.
  6. You can select an image and create variations of it or edit it further using text instructions.
  7. You may have a limited number of free credits to start with, and you might need to purchase more credits to generate more images.
  8. Download the images you create.

Paid/Free Status

DALL-E 2 operates on a credit system. Users typically receive a certain number of free credits upon signup and can purchase additional credits as needed. The cost per image generation depends on the resolution.
