VideoProc

VideoProc is a comprehensive and user-friendly video processing software that integrates a video editor, converter, downloader, recorder, and media player into one powerful tool. It's designed to handle various video tasks efficiently, even with large 4K and HD videos.

Uses of VideoProc

  • Editing videos: trimming, cutting, merging, splitting, rotating, cropping, adding subtitles, effects, and watermarks.
  • Converting video and audio formats between a wide range of codecs (e.g., MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, HEVC/H.265, H.264, MP3, AAC).
  • Resizing and adjusting video resolution, bitrate, and frame rate.
  • Downloading videos and music from various online platforms (e.g., YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook).
  • Recording computer screen and webcam with options for annotations and highlighting.
  • Stabilizing shaky video footage.
  • Removing fisheye lens distortion.
  • Forcing A/V sync.
  • Creating GIFs from videos.
  • Extracting audio from video files.

How to Use VideoProc

  1. Go to the VideoProc website and download and install the software. They often offer a free trial version.
  2. Purchase a license if you want to unlock all features and remove limitations.
  3. Open VideoProc. You'll see a main interface with different modules for Video, DVD, Downloader, Recorder, and Toolbox.
  4. **Video Editing:** Click the "Video" button to access the editing interface. Import your video files and use the various tools at the bottom and top to perform edits like trim, cut, effect, watermark, subtitle, etc.
  5. **Video Converting:** Click the "Video" button, import your video(s), and then select the desired output format and codec at the bottom. Adjust parameters like resolution, bitrate, and audio settings if needed, and click "Run".
  6. **Video Downloading:** Click the "Downloader" button, add the URL of the online video you want to download, analyze it, select the desired resolution and format, and click "Download Selected Videos".
  7. **Screen Recording:** Click the "Recorder" button and choose "Screen Recorder" or "Webcam Recorder". Configure the recording area, audio source, and other settings, then click "REC" to start recording.
  8. **Toolbox:** Explore the "Toolbox" for additional features like video stabilization, fisheye correction, GIF maker, etc. Select the tool you need and follow the on-screen instructions.
  9. After completing your tasks in each module, you can save or export your processed video or audio.

Paid/Free Status

VideoProc offers a **free trial version** with some limitations (e.g., a watermark on exported videos, limited conversion time). To unlock all features and remove these limitations, you need to purchase a **license**. They offer different license options for personal and family use, often with lifetime upgrades available.
