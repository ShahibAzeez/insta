Leonardo AI

Leonardo AI is a generative AI platform focused on creating high-quality images and art assets. It offers a range of models and tools designed for artists, designers, and creators to bring their visions to life with AI.

Uses of Leonardo AI

  • Generating realistic images and photorealistic artwork
  • Creating unique and stylized art in various artistic styles
  • Developing concept art and visual prototypes
  • Generating game assets and textures
  • Transforming sketches into polished artwork (image-to-image)
  • Creating variations and edits of existing images
  • Generating images for marketing materials and social media

How to Use Leonardo AI

  1. Go to the Leonardo AI website and create an account or log in. You might initially need to join a waitlist.
  2. Once you have access, explore the platform's interface, which includes various AI models and tools.
  3. **Text to Image:** Navigate to the "AI Image Generation" section and enter a detailed text prompt describing the image you want to create.
  4. Select a base model from the available options (e.g., Leonardo Diffusion, Stable Diffusion).
  5. Adjust parameters such as:
    • **Aspect Ratio:** Choose the desired dimensions of your image.
    • **Number of Images:** Specify how many variations you want to generate.
    • **Guidance Scale:** Control how closely the AI follows your prompt.
    • **ControlNet (if available):** Use input images to guide the generation process.
    • **Negative Prompts:** Specify elements you want to avoid in the generated image.
  6. Click the "Generate" button to create your image(s).
  7. **Image to Image:** Upload an existing image and use a text prompt to guide the AI in creating variations or transformations.
  8. Explore other tools and features like "Canvas Editor" for inpainting and outpainting, and the ability to train your own custom AI models.
  9. Browse the community feed for inspiration and to see what other users are creating.

Paid/Free Status

Leonardo AI typically offers a free tier with a limited number of "fast generation" credits that refresh daily. For more extensive use and access to more features, they offer various paid subscription plans with higher credit allowances and additional benefits.
