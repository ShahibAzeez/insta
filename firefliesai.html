Fireflies.ai

Fireflies.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that helps you transcribe, summarize, and analyze voice conversations from meetings across various platforms.

Uses of Fireflies.ai

  • Automatically transcribing meetings and voice notes
  • Summarizing key discussion points and action items
  • Analyzing meeting sentiment and speaker talk time
  • Searching meeting transcripts for specific keywords
  • Collaborating on meeting notes and highlights
  • Integrating with popular meeting platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, etc.)
  • Creating audio snippets and sharing meeting moments

How to Use Fireflies.ai

  1. Go to the Fireflies.ai website.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Connect your calendar and meeting platforms to Fireflies.ai.
  4. Invite the Fireflies.ai bot to your meetings, or it may automatically join based on your settings.
  5. During the meeting, Fireflies.ai will record and transcribe the audio.
  6. After the meeting, you will receive a transcript and summary in your Fireflies.ai dashboard.
  7. Review the transcript, highlights, and action items.
  8. Use the search functionality to find specific information.
  9. Share meeting notes and collaborate with team members.
  10. Customize settings for transcription languages, summaries, and integrations.

Paid/Free Status

Fireflies.ai offers a free plan with limited transcription minutes and features. They also have various paid subscription plans that provide higher transcription limits, access to advanced features like sentiment analysis and custom summaries, and team collaboration tools.
