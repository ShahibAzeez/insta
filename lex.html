Lex (by Every)

Lex is an AI-powered writing assistant from Every, designed to help you think, write, and create more effectively. It combines a clean writing interface with powerful AI capabilities.

Uses of Lex AI

  • Generating ideas and overcoming writer's block
  • Drafting articles, essays, and other long-form content
  • Summarizing text and extracting key points
  • Rewriting and refining existing content
  • Brainstorming headlines and titles
  • Creating outlines and structuring documents
  • Assisting with research and information gathering

How to Use Lex AI

  1. Go to the Lex AI website.
  2. Sign up for an account or log in.
  3. Start a new document in the Lex editor.
  4. Begin writing as you normally would.
  5. Use the AI commands and features integrated into the editor. This might involve highlighting text and selecting an AI action, or using specific prompts.
  6. Experiment with different AI functions to generate text, summarize, rewrite, or brainstorm.
  7. Review and edit the AI-generated content to fit your needs and style.
  8. Continue writing and using AI assistance as you develop your document.

Paid/Free Status

Lex AI is currently in a beta or early access phase. Information regarding its paid or free status may vary. It's best to check the official Lex website for the most up-to-date details on pricing and subscription plans.
