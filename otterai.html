Otter.ai

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that transcribes audio in real-time, generates meeting summaries, and facilitates collaboration on meeting notes.

Uses of Otter.ai

  • Real-time transcription of meetings and voice conversations
  • Generating automated meeting summaries and action items
  • Recording and transcribing audio notes and lectures
  • Searching and highlighting keywords within transcripts
  • Collaborating on meeting notes with team members
  • Integrating with popular meeting platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, etc.)
  • Analyzing speaker talk time and identifying key topics
  • Creating custom vocabulary for improved transcription accuracy

How to Use Otter.ai

  1. Go to the Otter.ai website and create an account or log in.
  2. Connect your calendar and meeting platforms to Otter.ai.
  3. For scheduled meetings, Otter.ai can automatically join and record/transcribe. You may need to invite the Otter.ai bot to your meetings.
  4. You can also record directly within the Otter.ai web interface or mobile app.
  5. During the meeting or recording, Otter.ai will transcribe the audio in real-time.
  6. After the meeting, review and edit the transcript for accuracy.
  7. Utilize the AI Summary feature to get a quick overview of the meeting highlights and action items.
  8. Share meeting notes and collaborate with team members by inviting them to view or edit the transcript.
  9. Search for specific keywords or phrases within the transcripts.
  10. Organize your recordings and transcripts into folders.
  11. Customize settings for language, speaker identification, and integrations.

Paid/Free Status

Otter.ai offers a free plan with limited transcription minutes per month. They also have various paid subscription plans (Pro, Business, Enterprise) that offer higher transcription limits, longer recording durations, advanced features like custom vocabulary and analytics, and team collaboration tools.
