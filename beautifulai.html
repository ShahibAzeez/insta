Beautiful.ai

Beautiful.ai is an AI-powered presentation software that helps you create visually stunning and professional presentations in a fraction of the time. Its smart slide templates adapt automatically as you add content, ensuring consistent design.

Uses of Beautiful.ai

  • Creating professional business presentations
  • Designing pitch decks and sales presentations
  • Developing educational and training materials
  • Generating visually appealing reports and data visualizations
  • Collaborating with teams on presentations in real-time
  • Maintaining brand consistency across all presentations
  • Quickly iterating on presentation design and layout

How to Use Beautiful.ai

  1. Go to the Beautiful.ai website and create an account or log in.
  2. Start a new presentation from scratch or choose from a variety of smart templates.
  3. Add slides and content (text, images, charts, videos) to the pre-designed templates.
  4. The AI-powered Smart Slide system automatically adjusts the layout and design as you add content, ensuring visual harmony.
  5. Customize the look and feel with your brand colors and fonts.
  6. Utilize the built-in library of icons and images.
  7. Collaborate with team members by sharing presentations and allowing for real-time editing.
  8. Present directly from the platform or export your presentation in various formats (e.g., PDF, PowerPoint).

Paid/Free Status

Beautiful.ai is a subscription-based service. They offer various paid plans with different features and for different team sizes. They typically provide a free trial period to allow users to explore the platform before committing to a subscription. They do not usually offer a completely free plan for ongoing use.
